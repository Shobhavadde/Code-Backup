Intelligent Hiring

No more candidate offer drop surprises

A simple solution to enable you know if your candidates are offer shopping so you can be prepared with Plan B to ensure your business & clients don’t get impacted.

The Challenge

Multiple offers:

Candidates are taking multiple offers with serveral companies and dropping aout. Employers have no way of knowing about candidates’ offer-shopping.

Dropout Rate:

Offer drop out rate (including at senior levels) is between 30-50% across the board; goes upto 60% in some functions and is usually in the few weeks before joining.

Business outage

Collateral impact on business & client – For corporates, delayed resourcing causes client outage and for search consultants its a loss of revenue.

The Solution

Centralized SaaS Platform:

A cloud based, secure tech platform that hosts candidates offer details across companies & industries. – For potential employers & search consultants.

Check For Existing Offers With A Candidate:

Prior to scheduling interviews or offer rollout, members check for an existing offer with the candidate; make an informed decision on whether to proceed with the offer or not.

Upload Offer Acceptances:

Members upload unique identifiers for every candidate offer once it has been accepted by the candidate to the central repository to enable other members view any duplicate offers.
